Spreading Quiz Bowl Across California

We help schools, clubs, and students discover quiz bowl, a fast-paced academic team competition that rewards breadth of knowledge, teamwork, and quick recall. Our focus is accessibility: low-cost tournaments, coach training, and outreach to new schools and aspiring teams statewide.

Our mission

The California Quiz Bowl League (CQBL) exists to expand access to competitive academic activities for students across California. We believe quiz bowl develops research skills, teamwork, public speaking confidence, and intellectual curiosity. We prioritize:

  • Inclusion
    Bring quiz bowl to schools with limited resources.
  • Education
    Training, tips, and practice.
  • Community
    Peer networks, mentor coaches, and inter-school tournaments.

What we run

Tournaments

We host beginner-friendly and open tournaments throughout the calendar year. Events are run with clear rulesets, provided moderators/referees, and strive to keep entry costs minimal.

Outreach

We're dedicated to growing quiz bowl across California by helping schools, teachers, and students discover the excitement of academic competition. We work to support new programs in any way we can

Resources for Teams & Coaches

Here are some essential tools and guides to help you start or improve your quiz bowl program:

  • How to Start a Team (NAQT) — step-by-step instructions on forming, funding, and growing a quiz bowl team.
  • QBReader — play real tossups online, practice solo, or compete with friends in real time.
  • Quiz Bowl Wiki: How to Study — community-curated study strategies and topic guides.
  • hsquizbowl.org Database — forums, past tournaments, and searchable quiz bowl archives.
  • NAQT “You Gotta Know” Study Guides — concise overviews of common quiz bowl topics.
